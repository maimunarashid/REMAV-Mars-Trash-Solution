Cosmic Celebration

Fabric Scrapes to soft wall hangings

Previous Use

  • Thermal clothing for crew members
  • Gloves and socks for EVA and indoor wear
  • Soft padding in sleeping quarters

Why It's Now Trash

After extended use, the fabric becomes torn, stretched, or contaminated. It’s no longer suitable for wear but still holds reusable material value.

Processing Steps

  1. Clean with disinfectant wipes
  2. Cut into strips, squares, or decorative shapes
  3. Stitch or tie using thread or wire
  4. Hang across habitat walls or ceilings

Output

Colorful garlands, banners, and soft wall hangings made from reused fabric.

How Astronauts Use It

Used to decorate for crew birthdays, boost morale, and personalize living space — all without introducing harmful materials into the Martian habitat.
